Summary of our Facilities

All room prices include daily 5-star, classic 5-course Gourmet dinner in our European style dinning room or as room service brought to you. And classic style dinner in-bed.

Gastronomic 5 Course Meal: 1 amuse bouche, 2 Starters (either Appy, Soup or Salad), 1 main Entree Choice and 1 Dessert Choice.

Vegan and Vegetarian dishes are available, we offer a large array of choices from Heart Smart, Gluten Free and Celiac Disease on your request.

Dining Room Open - 6:00 pm to 8:00 pm (to 9:00 pm Fridays). Reserve Table and Place Order Between 12pm - 5pm. 866-202-1179 ext 108

Breakfast Room-Service Hours - 8:00 am to 10:30 am. Best to place with your dinner order. 866-202-1179 ext 102

Lunch Menu - Extra charge. Daily from 12:00 - 4:00 pm. 866-202-1179 ext 108

Patio Service - Lunch Only- Please wait to be seated as the tables need to be sanitize.
